# Tasnimilion

**Tasnimilion** = 10^(10³⁰)  
That’s a **1 followed by one nonillion zeros**.

---

## 🧠 Concept

Tasnimilion continues the grand journey into the world of incomprehensible quantities. Numbers like this challenge our understanding of scale and time.
